Source of our data: We took the data about Airline Passengers from Kaggle.
You can Access it using this link: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/iamsouravbanerjee/airline-dataset

Passenger ID(Integer):  A unique identifier assigned to each passenger.
First Name(String):  The first name of the passenger.
Last Name(String):  The last name of the passenger.
Gender(String):  The gender of the passenger (e.g., Male, Female, Non-binary, etc.).
Age(Integer):  The age of the passenger.
Nationality(String):  The nationality of the passenger (i.e., the country of citizenship)
Airport Name(String):  The name of the airport where the passenger's flight originates or departs
Departure Date(Date):  The date when the flight departs.
Arrival Airport(String):  The destination airport of the passenger's flight.
Flight Status(String):  The status of the flight (e.g., On Time, Delayed, Cancelled, etc.).
